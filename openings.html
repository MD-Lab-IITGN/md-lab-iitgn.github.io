Current Opportunities at Biomaterials and Drug Delivery Laboratory @ IIT Gandhinagar

  • We are pleased to host enthusiastic graduating PhD students (Thesis submitted or defended) as postdoctoral fellows through national schemes such as NPDF, DBT-RA, DST Nanomission PDF, DBT-IYBA, DBT/Wellcome Trust India Alliance, etc.
  • Students (with MSc/MTech/MS) interested in pursuing PhD in the area of Biomaterials, Drug Delivery, Regenerative Medicines are most welcome to apply through IIT Gandhinagar PhD admission portal.
  • MSc/MTech students with CSIR/UGC, DBT, ICMR, GATE are highly encouraged to apply.
  • IITGN provides international level housing and research facilities to research scholars and postdocs.
